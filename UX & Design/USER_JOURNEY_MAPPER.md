# 🗺️ User Journey Mapper

**Goal:** Map out the end-to-end user journey for a specific scenario, identifying key actions, emotional states, pain points, and opportunities.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as an expert UX Researcher. I am building a product/feature for:
"[INSERT PRODUCT/FEATURE - e.g., 'A mobile app for booking last-minute dog walkers']"

I want to map the user journey for the following persona:
"[INSERT PERSONA - e.g., 'A busy professional who just found out they have to stay late at work']"

The scenario starts when: "[INSERT TRIGGER - e.g., 'The user realizes they cannot walk their dog at the usual time']"
And ends when: "[INSERT RESOLUTION - e.g., 'The dog is returned home safely after the walk']"

Please generate a detailed step-by-step user journey map. For each distinct phase (e.g., Discovery, Consideration, Action, Post-Action), include:
1. **User Goal:** What are they trying to achieve in this step?
2. **Actions:** What specific steps do they take?
3. **Emotional State:** How are they feeling? (e.g., anxious, relieved, frustrated)
4. **Pain Points:** What friction might they encounter?
5. **Opportunities:** How can our product delight them or remove friction at this stage?

Present this in a clear, easy-to-read format.
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
Run this prompt *before* writing your PRD. The "Opportunities" section often highlights the most critical features you need to build to differentiate your product.
