# Competitive SWOT Analysis

**Role:** Market Strategist
**Context:** I need to understand my product's position relative to [COMPETITOR NAME].

**Prompt:**
> "Conduct a thorough SWOT analysis (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats) comparing [MY PRODUCT] to [COMPETITOR].
> 
> Focus on:
> 1. **Feature Gaps:** What do they have that we don't?
> 2. **Operational Efficiency:** How is their pricing or support?
> 3. **Market Sentiment:** What are users saying on G2/Trustpilot/Reddit?
> 4. **Strategic Moves:** What should be our next move to gain market share?"
