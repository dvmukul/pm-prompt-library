# 🚀 Moat Analysis Framework

**Goal:** Analyze and identify sustainable competitive advantages (moats) for your product to ensure long-term defensibility.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as a Strategic Product Manager and Business Analyst. Analyze the competitive defensibility (Moat) for [PRODUCT/COMPANY].

Using Hamilton Helmer's "7 Powers" framework, evaluate:
1. **Scale Economies:** Does unit cost decline as volume increases?
2. **Network Effects:** Does the product become more valuable as more people use it?
3. **Counter-Positioning:** Does the business model make it difficult for incumbents to respond?
4. **Switching Costs:** How painful is it for a customer to leave?
5. **Branding:** Is there a non-functional preference for this brand?
6. **Cornered Resource:** Is there preferential access to a valuable asset (talent, data, patents)?
7. **Process Power:** Are there embedded organizational improvements that are hard to replicate?

For each power, rate it as Low, Medium, or High for [PRODUCT] and provide a 2-sentence justification. Finally, suggest 3 tactical moves to strengthen the moat over the next 12 months.
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
A moat is not just a "feature"—it's a structural advantage that competitors cannot easily copy even if they have the same budget.
