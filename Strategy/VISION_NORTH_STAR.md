# Vision & North Star Metric

**Role:** Head of Product
**Context:** Our team needs a clear direction for the next 12-18 months.

**Prompt:**
> "Help me draft a product vision statement for [PRODUCT].
> 
> It should be:
> 1. **Inspirational:** Exciting for the team.
> 2. **Outcome-Oriented:** Focused on the value delivered, not features.
> 
> Additionally, propose 3 candidate 'North Star Metrics' that align with this vision. For each metric, explain why it's a 'leading indicator' of long-term success rather than a lagging revenue metric."
