# 🔍 Product Discovery Prompts

Focus on understanding user pain points, market opportunities, and problem definition.

## Prompts

### 1. [User Persona Deep Dive](./USER_PERSONA.md)
Generate detailed user personas including motivations, pain points, and workflows.

### 2. [Pain Point Extractor](./PAIN_POINT_EXTRACTOR.md)
Analyze raw user feedback to identify and categorize recurring problems.

### 3. [Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) Framework](./JTBD_FRAMEWORK.md)
Craft 'Jobs' statements to focus on the underlying needs of your users.
