# User Persona Deep Dive

**Role:** Expert UX Researcher / Product Manager
**Context:** I am developing [PRODUCT NAME] for [TARGET AUDIENCE].

**Prompt:**
> "I want you to act as an expert UX Researcher. Create 3 detailed user personas for [PRODUCT/FEATURE]. 
> For each persona, include:
> 1. **Bio & Role:** A brief background.
> 2. **Core Goals:** What are they trying to achieve?
> 3. **Primary Pain Points:** What frustrates them about the current process?
> 4. **Daily Workflow:** How does this product fit into their day?
> 5. **Success Criteria:** What does a 'win' look like for them?
> 
> Format as a clean table or bulleted list."
