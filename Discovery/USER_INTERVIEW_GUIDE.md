# 🔍 User Interview Guide Generator

**Goal:** Generate a structured, non-biased user interview script to validate hypotheses and uncover deep user needs.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as an expert User Researcher and Senior Product Manager. I am working on [PRODUCT/FEATURE] for [TARGET PERSONA]. 

My core hypothesis is: [INSERT HYPOTHESIS - e.g., "Users struggle to track their expenses across multiple apps"].

Generate a structured user interview guide (30-45 mins) that includes:
1. **Introduction & Context Setting:** How to put the user at ease.
2. **Warm-up Questions:** 2-3 questions about their current workflow/behavior.
3. **Deep Dive (Problem Discovery):** 5-7 open-ended, non-leading questions focusing on their past experiences (not future predictions). Use the "Five Whys" technique where appropriate.
4. **Hypothesis Testing:** Subtle ways to validate or invalidate the hypothesis without asking "Would you use this?".
5. **Wrap-up:** Final questions and next steps.

Guidelines:
- Avoid leading questions.
- Focus on "Tell me about a time when..."
- Prioritize understanding the "Pain" and "Value".
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
Record the session (with permission) and look for emotional cues (frustration, excitement) rather than just the words they say.
