# 📊 Metric Trees (KPI Decomposition)

**Goal:** Decompose a high-level North Star Metric into actionable input metrics to understand the "levers" of your business.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as a Senior Product Analyst and Head of Product. My North Star Metric is [INSERT NSM - e.g., 'Weekly Active Subscribers'].

Generate a Metric Tree that decomposes this NSM into L1, L2, and L3 metrics.

Format:
- **North Star Metric:** [Metric Name]
  - **L1 Metric (Core Driver):** [Metric Name]
    - **L2 Metric (Sub-driver):** [Metric Name]
      - **L3 Metric (Tactical Lever):** [Metric Name]

For each L1 and L2 metric, provide:
- **Definition:** How is it calculated?
- **Why it matters:** How does it influence the North Star?
- **Owner:** Which team (e.g., Growth, Engagement, Platform) likely owns this?

Focus on making the metrics "Actionable" and "Measurable".
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
A good metric tree helps you explain *why* your North Star is moving by looking at the sub-drivers.
