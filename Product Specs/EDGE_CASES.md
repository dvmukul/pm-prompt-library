# Edge Case Brainstormer

**Role:** Product Quality Specialist
**Context:** We are finalising the spec for [FEATURE] and want to avoid surprises during QA or after launch.

**Prompt:**
> "I am designing [FEATURE/WORKFLOW]. 
> The happy path is: [DESCRIBE HAPPY PATH].
> 
> Please brainstorm 10 potential edge cases or 'unhappy paths' we should consider, categorized by:
> 1. **User Errors:** What can a user do wrong?
> 2. **System Failures:** Network drops, API timeouts, or data corruption.
> 3. **Concurrency Issues:** Multiple users or multiple devices.
> 4. **Security/Privacy:** Malicious inputs or unauthorized access.
> 
> For each edge case, suggest a 'Design Fix' or 'Graceful Degradation' strategy."
