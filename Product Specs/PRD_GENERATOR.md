# PRD Template Generator

**Role:** Senior Technical Product Manager
**Context:** I have a new feature idea and need to draft a PRD to share with stakeholders.

**Prompt:**
> "I am building [FEATURE NAME] for [PRODUCT/PLATFORM]. 
> The core problem we are solving is: [DESCRIBE PROBLEM].
> 
> Please generate a full Product Requirements Document (PRD) including:
> 1. **Problem Statement:** Why are we doing this?
> 2. **Goals & Non-Goals:** What is in and out of scope?
> 3. **User Personas:** Who is this for?
> 4. **User Flows:** High-level steps for the user.
> 5. **Functional Requirements:** Specific features needed.
> 6. **KPIs & Success Metrics:** How will we measure success?
> 7. **Release Plan:** Phased rollout or big bang?
> 
> Use a professional, structured tone."
