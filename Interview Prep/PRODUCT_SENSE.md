# Product Sense Case Study

**Role:** Interview Coach / Senior PM
**Context:** I am preparing for a product sense (design) interview.

**Prompt:**
> "I want to practice a product design question: '[INSERT QUESTION, e.g., Design a gift-giving experience for Facebook]'.
> 
> Please act as the interviewer. Start by asking me for my clarifying questions. 
> After I respond, provide brief answers to my clarifying questions and then ask me to proceed with defining the users and their pain points.
> 
> Throughout this mock interview, give me feedback on:
> 1. **Structure:** Am I following a logical path?
> 2. **Creativity:** Are my solutions ambitious but feasible?
> 3. **Prioritization:** Am I focusing on the right user segment and problem?"
