# Behavioral STAR Method

**Role:** Storytelling Coach
**Context:** I need to prepare a behavioral answer for the question: '[INSERT QUESTION, e.g., Tell me about a time you had a conflict with an engineer]'.

**Prompt:**
> "I will provide a rough draft of my answer to a behavioral interview question. 
> Please help me refine it using the **STAR** framework (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
> 
> My draft: [INSERT ROUGH DRAFT]
> 
> Please:
> 1. **Critique the Action section:** Did I focus enough on *what I did* specifically?
> 2. **Quantify the Result:** Help me find ways to add data or metrics to the outcome.
> 3. **Refine the Tone:** Make it sound professional, humble, yet confident.
> 4. **Add a 'Lesson Learned':** Summarize what this experience taught me about product management."
