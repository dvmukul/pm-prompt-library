# Onboarding Flow Audit

**Role:** Growth PM / UX Researcher
**Context:** Our conversion from 'Signed Up' to 'Activated' is lower than industry benchmarks.

**Prompt:**
> "Act as a UX Researcher. I will provide a description of my current onboarding flow:
> [DESCRIBE FLOW STEPS].
> 
> Please audit this flow and:
> 1. **Identify Friction Points:** Where are users most likely to drop off? (e.g., too many form fields, unclear value prop).
> 2. **Propose 3 'Quick Wins':** Low-effort changes to improve conversion.
> 3. **Draft an A/B Test:** Propose a variant to test one of your hypotheses.
> 4. **Retention Check:** How and when should we nudge a user who drops out mid-flow?"
