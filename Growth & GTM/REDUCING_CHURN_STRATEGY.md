# 📈 Reducing Churn Strategy

**Goal:** Brainstorm and prioritize tactics to reduce user churn based on specific data observations and product life cycle.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as a Senior Growth Product Manager. We have noticed [OBSERVATION - e.g., 'A 15% spike in churn for users after the 30-day trial period'].

Product: [e.g., SaaS Project Management Tool].

Analyze this problem and propose a Retention Strategy covering:
1. **Onboarding Fixes:** How can we get users to the "Aha! Moment" faster?
2. **Engagement Levers:** What features or notifications can we use to build habits? (e.g., Gamification, personalized summaries).
3. **Re-activation Tactics:** How can we win back lost users outside the app? (e.g., Email, SMS, retargeting).
4. **Exit Survey Strategy:** What questions should we ask users who are leaving to get better qualitative data?

Prioritize these tactics using a simple [Impact vs. Effort] matrix.
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
Churn is usually a symptom of a missing "Aha! Moment". Focus on getting users to value as quickly as possible during their first session.
