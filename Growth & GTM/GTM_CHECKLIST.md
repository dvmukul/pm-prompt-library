# GTM Launch Checklist

**Role:** Product Operations
**Context:** [FEATURE] is launching in 4 weeks. I need a tight checklist to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

**Prompt:**
> "Help me build a Go-To-Market (GTM) checklist for the launch of [FEATURE].
> 
> Include columns for **Task**, **Owner**, **Timeline**, and **Status**.
> 
> Categorize tasks into:
> 1. **Product:** Final QA, internal dogfooding, release notes.
> 2. **Marketing:** Landing page update, email blast, social media posts.
> 3. **Sales/Success:** Pitch deck update, internal training, help center articles.
> 4. **Legal/Privacy:** Terms of Service updates, privacy review.
> 
> Suggest realistic deadlines based on a 'T-minus 4 weeks' timeline."
