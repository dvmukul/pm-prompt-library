# Technical Discovery Questions

**Role:** Technical Product Manager
**Context:** I have a meeting with the Lead Engineer and Architect to discuss the feasibility of [NEW FEATURE/SYSTEM].

**Prompt:**
> "I am conducting technical discovery for [FEATURE NAME]. 
> High-level goal: [DESCRIBE].
> 
> Please generate a list of 10 'Critical Discovery Questions' for my engineers. 
> Categorize them into:
> 1. **Feasibility:** Can we build this with our current stack?
> 2. **Scalability:** How will this perform at 10x our current load?
> 3. **Observability:** How will we monitor success and failure?
> 4. **Dependencies:** What internal/external services do we need?
> 5. **Security/Privacy:** Are there data handling risks?
> 
> For each question, provide a 'PM Translation' explaining *why* I should care about the answer."
