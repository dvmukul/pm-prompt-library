# Architecture Review Prep

**Role:** Senior PM (System Design Focus)
**Context:** I'm attending an RFC (Request for Comments) meeting for a major system update.

**Prompt:**
> "I am reviewing a technical RFC for [SYSTEM/ARCHITECTURE CHANGE].
> Goal: [DESCRIBE].
> 
> Act as a critical but constructive 'Technical PM Reviewer'. Generate a list of feedback points focused on **Product Impact**:
> 1. **Implementation Effort vs. Value:** Does the complexity justify the outcome?
> 2. **Extensibility:** Will this architecture lock us in, or can we add [FUTURE FEATURE] later?
> 3. **Maintenance Burden:** Will this require significant 'on-call' support?
> 4. **User-Facing Latency:** How will this design impact page load or response times?
> 5. **Rollback Strategy:** If this fails, how easily can we revert without data loss?"
