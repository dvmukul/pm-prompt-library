# Feature Launch Internal FAQ

**Role:** Product Operations
**Context:** We are launching [FEATURE] and need to prepare our support and sales teams.

**Prompt:**
> "I am launching [FEATURE NAME] on [DATE]. 
> Key benefits include: [BENEFIT 1, BENEFIT 2].
> Target audience: [AUDIENCE].
> 
> Please generate a list of 10 'Frequently Asked Questions' that Sales or Support teams might get from users. 
> For each question, provide:
> 1. **The Question:** (e.g., 'Will this be available on mobile?')
> 2. **The Internal Answer:** Detailed explanation for the team.
> 3. **The Customer-Facing Response:** A concise, polite, and approved answer to share with users."
