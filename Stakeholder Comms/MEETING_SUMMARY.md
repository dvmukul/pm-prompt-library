# Meeting Summary & Actions

**Role:** Product Manager (Efficiency Expert)
**Context:** I just finished a meeting about [TOPIC] and have a rough transcript or notes.

**Prompt:**
> "Please summarize the following meeting notes/transcript:
> 
> [TRANSCRIPT OR NOTES]
> 
> Organize the summary into:
> 1. **Meeting Objective:** Was the goal achieved?
> 2. **Key Decisions Made:** What was formally agreed upon?
> 3. **Action Items:** List by owner and deadline (if mentioned).
> 4. **Parking Lot:** Topics that were deferred for later.
> 
> Format as a clean markdown document suitable for pasting into Slack or Confluence."
