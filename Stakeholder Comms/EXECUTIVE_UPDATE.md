# Executive Status Update

**Role:** Product Lead
**Context:** I need to send a weekly or monthly update to the VP of Product.

**Prompt:**
> "Draft an executive status update for [PROJECT/PRODUCT].
> Use the following format:
> 1. **The 'TL;DR':** 1-2 sentences on current health.
> 2. **Key Milestones Achieved:** Top 3 wins since the last update.
> 3. **The 'So What?':** What is the business impact of these wins?
> 4. **Roadblocks & Risks:** What is slowing us down or could go wrong?
> 5. **Next Steps:** What are we focusing on next?
> 
> Keep it punchy, use bullet points, and avoid technical jargon."
