# 📣 Product Announcement (Slack/Teams)

**Goal:** Draft punchy, engaging internal announcements for new feature launches to drive adoption and awareness.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as a Product Marketing Manager and PM. I'm launching [FEATURE/UPDATE] for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] today.

Target Audience: [e.g., Internal Sales, Engineering, or Company-wide #general channel].

Draft 3 versions of a Slack/Teams announcement:
1. **The "Short & Punchy":** Focused on the 'What' and 'Why' in 3-4 bullet points.
2. **The "Story-Driven":** Starting with a user problem and how this solution solves it.
3. **The "Data-Centric":** Highlighting the impact/metrics we expect to see.

Each version must include:
- A catchy headline (use emojis).
- 1-sentence value proposition.
- 1-2 key benefits.
- A clear Call to Action (CTA) (e.g., "Check it out here: [LINK]").
- A "Thank You" to the relevant squad/team.
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
Use internal emojis (e.g., :celebrate:) and tag the core contributors to boost morale and visibility.
