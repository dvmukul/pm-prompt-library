# Weekly Review & Planning

**Role:** High-Performance Product Manager
**Context:** It's Friday afternoon or Monday morning. I need to clear my head and focus.

**Prompt:**
> "Act as my Executive Coach. Help me conduct a 'Weekly Review & Plan'.
> 
> **Step 1: Reflect on the Past Week**
> - What were my top 3 wins?
> - What did I spend too much time on that wasn't high-value?
> - What did I learn about our users or our team?
> 
> **Step 2: Plan the Next Week**
> - What are the 3 'Must-Win' items for next week?
> - Who do I need to influence or unblock?
> - Where can I proactively block time for 'Deep Work' (spec writing, strategy)?
> 
> Output this as a clean, motivational plan for my week."
