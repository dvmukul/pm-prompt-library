# ⚡ Email Co-pilot (Stakeholder Ghostwriting)

**Goal:** Draft clear, professional, and action-oriented stakeholder emails or clarify complex, multi-party email threads.

---

### 📝 The Prompt

```text
Act as an Executive Assistant and Communication Lead for a Senior PM. 

Context: [PASTE EMAIL THREAD OR BRUTALLY HONEST DRAFT HERE].

I need to [GOAL - e.g., 'Update them that the launch is delayed by 2 weeks due to a critical bug without sounding defensive'].

Generate 2 options for this email:
1. **The "Direct & Professional":** Clear, data-driven, and focused on the path forward.
2. **The "Empathetic & Collaborative":** Acknowledging the impact on their teams and inviting a sync.

Each draft must include:
- A clear, descriptive Subject Line.
- A "TL;DR" (Too Long; Didn't Read) summary at the top.
- The core news (Good or Bad).
- Clearly defined "Next Steps" or "Actions Required" from the recipient.
- A signature that invites feedback.

Tone: [e.g., Confident but humble].
```

---

### 💡 Pro-Tip
Never send an email when you're angry. Use this prompt to "translate" your frustration into a professional, productive message.
