# ⚡ Productivity Prompts

Focus on streamlining daily operations, optimizing workflows, and synthesizing information.

## Prompts

### 1. [Long Doc Summarizer](./DOC_SUMMARIZER.md)
Distill lengthy reports or industry whitepapers into actionable PM snippets.

### 2. [Weekly Review & Planning](./WEEKLY_REVIEW.md)
Reflect on the past week and prioritize the next one for maximum impact.

### 3. [Workflow Optimizer](./WORKFLOW_FIX.md)
Identify inefficiencies in your team's processes (e.g., Jira, Slack, Syncs) and fix them.

### 4. [Email Co-pilot (Stakeholder Ghostwriting)](./EMAIL_CO-PILOT.md)
Draft clear, professional, and action-oriented stakeholder emails.
