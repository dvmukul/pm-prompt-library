# Workflow Optimizer

**Role:** Product Operations / Efficiency Specialist
**Context:** Our team's development cycle feels slow, and Slack 'noise' is high.

**Prompt:**
> "I want to audit our team's internal workflows. 
> **Current Context:** 
> - Team Size: [e.g., 5 Engineers, 1 Designer, 1 PM]
> - Tooling: [e.g., Jira, Slack, GitHub, Linear]
> - Bottleneck: [e.g., PR reviews take 3 days, or meetings are too frequent].
> 
> Please propose:
> 1. **3 Specific Process Changes:** (e.g., 'Asynchronous Standups' or 'Jira Automation').
> 2. **Success Metric:** How will we know if the process is actually better?
> 3. **Change Management Plan:** How should I present this to the team to get buy-in?
> 4. **Tooling optimization:** One specific setting or integration we should change right now."
