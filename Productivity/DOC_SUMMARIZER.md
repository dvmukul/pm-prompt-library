# Long Doc Summarizer

**Role:** Information Architect / PM
**Context:** I have a 50-page industry report or internal strategy doc that I need to digest quickly.

**Prompt:**
> "Please summarize the following document:
> 
> [INSERT TEXT OR LINK TO CONTENT]
> 
> High-level summary requirements:
> 1. **The Executive TL;DR:** 3 sentences on the 'What' and 'Why'.
> 2. **3 Key Insights:** What are the most surprising or important findings?
> 3. **Impact on our Product:** How does this relate to [MY PROJECT]?
> 4. **Actionable Recommendations:** What should we do next based on this?
> 5. **Follow-up Questions:** What data is missing or what should I ask the author?"
